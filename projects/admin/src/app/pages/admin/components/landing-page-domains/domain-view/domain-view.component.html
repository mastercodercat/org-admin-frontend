How to Verify Your Domain

You may need help from your system administrator to complete this step.

To verify a domain, you will need ownership or administrative access to your domain name. You will need to access and log into your domain registrar(or DNS provider) for your domain, e.g. GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc. For further instructions on setting up your domain, including links to documentation of common domain registrars, please check our knowledge base.

Add the following record to your domain's DNS zone file to verify your ownership of this domain. After you've completed this step through your DNS provider, click "Verify Now" in Helm.

It may take up to 24 hours for the changes to take effect. If after 24 hours, you are still unable to successfully verify your domain, please contact us.
